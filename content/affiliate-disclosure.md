## How ChiFi Wire makes money

ChiFi Wire is reader-supported. Most "Where to buy" links on this site are **affiliate links** — when you click one and complete a purchase at the retailer, ChiFi Wire receives a small commission **at no extra cost to you**. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go to the retailer directly.

These commissions are how we fund the site (hosting, software, time spent writing). Without them, ChiFi Wire wouldn't exist.

## Programs we participate in

ChiFi Wire is a participant in (or has applied to) the following affiliate programs:

- **Amazon Associates** — commission on products purchased through Amazon links
- **Linsoul** — commission on ChiFi audio products purchased through Linsoul
- **HiFiGo** — commission on ChiFi audio products purchased through HiFiGo
- **Apos Audio** — commission on products purchased through Apos
- **ShenzhenAudio** — commission on products purchased through Shenzhen Audio
- **Various manufacturer direct programs** (Moondrop, Truthear, FiiO, etc.) where available

If you click a link to **any other retailer** not listed above, the link is provided for your convenience and we do **not** earn a commission.

## How this affects our content

We commit to the following editorial principles:

1. **We never accept payment for positive reviews.** Manufacturers do not pay us to write favorably about their products. If a manufacturer provides a review sample, we'll disclose it at the start of the article.
2. **Affiliate commission does not influence our recommendations.** We recommend products we'd buy ourselves, regardless of which retailer carries them or which affiliate program pays better.
3. **We disclose review samples.** If we receive a product for free or at a discount from a manufacturer for review, the article will say so.
4. **Negative reviews stay negative.** If a product doesn't deserve recommendation, we say so — even if the manufacturer participates in an affiliate program with us.
5. **Comparison articles are based on merit, not commission rate.** If two products are direct competitors and one pays a higher commission, that does not affect which one we recommend.

## What this means for you

- Clicking our affiliate link costs you **nothing extra**. Retail price is the same.
- You're under **no obligation** to use our links. We're happy if our content helps you make a buying decision, even if you buy elsewhere.
- If you want to **directly support ChiFi Wire**, using our links is the simplest way — no donations or memberships required.

## FTC compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the **U.S. Federal Trade Commission's 16 CFR Part 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising"**. Individual articles also include disclosure language at the point where affiliate links appear.
