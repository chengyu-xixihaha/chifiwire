*Last updated: June 23, 2026*

ChiFi Wire ("we", "us", or "our") operates chifiwire.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

**Automatically collected data.** When you visit chifiwire.com, standard web server logs and third-party analytics tools may collect:

- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URL
- General geographic location (country/region, not precise location)
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

## Cookies

ChiFi Wire uses cookies for the following purposes:

**Analytics.** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics sets cookies to track sessions and page views. You can opt out via [Google's opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

**Advertising.** We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

**Affiliate links.** Some links on this site are affiliate links (Amazon, Linsoul, HiFiGo, Apos Audio, and others). Clicking these links may set cookies from those third parties. We earn a small commission if you make a purchase, at no extra cost to you.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set their own cookies or collect data:

- **Google Analytics** — traffic analysis ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google AdSense** — advertising ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Cloudflare** — CDN and DDoS protection ([Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))

These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

## How We Use Information

We use collected data solely to:

- Understand which content is useful to readers
- Improve site navigation and content
- Serve relevant advertisements

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

Server logs are retained for up to 90 days. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' default retention settings (14 months).

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

For requests or questions, contact us at: **hello@chifiwire.com**

**EU/EEA residents (GDPR):** You have the right to withdraw consent to cookie-based tracking at any time. Use the opt-out links above or clear your browser cookies.

**California residents (CCPA):** We do not sell personal information. For CCPA inquiries, contact hello@chifiwire.com.

## Children's Privacy

ChiFi Wire is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
