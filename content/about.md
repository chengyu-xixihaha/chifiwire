ChiFi Wire covers Chinese hi-fi audio gear — IEMs, headphones, DAPs, DACs, and amplifiers — for English-speaking audiophiles.

We focus on the brands that have made "ChiFi" a category of its own: **Moondrop**, **Truthear**, **Letshuoer**, **FiiO**, **Topping**, **SMSL**, **HiBy**, **Tanchjim**, **KZ**, **TinHiFi**, and the constant stream of newcomers from Shenzhen and beyond.

## What you'll find

- **Reviews** — single-product deep-dives with sound impressions, fit notes, and verdict
- **Buying Guides** — best picks by budget and use case
- **Comparisons** — head-to-head matchups of similar gear
- **News** — fresh launches from Chinese manufacturers, before they hit Western retailers

## Editorial principles

- Honest impressions; no manufacturer payola
- Specs and pricing pulled from official sources where possible
- Recommendations grouped by clear use case (not generic "best of" filler)
- Affiliate links disclosed clearly (we may earn a commission, no extra cost to you)
